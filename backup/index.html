Biography

I am doing machine learning research at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. Le Song. I received a M.S. from Peking University and a B.Eng from South China University of Technology, both with highest honors.

My research interest broadly lies in machine learning, (deep) representation learning and their applications to computer vision. I extremely appreciate the beauty of “light-and-sweet” algorithms: (i) light: easy to implement in practice, (ii) sweet: having clear intuitions or non-trivial guarantees. I was fortunate to work closely with Yandong Wen, Zhiding Yu, and Prof. Meng Yang and Prof. Yuexian Zou.

My motto: Obstacles do not exist to stop us. They give us a chance to show how badly we want something.
