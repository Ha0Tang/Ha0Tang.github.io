Biography

My name is Hao Tang, I have graduated from Peking University and got my master’s degree in July, 2016. I used to study in the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering with Computer Application Technology being my major.

 

Most of my research over the past three years has significantly driven me use machine learning to work on the development of better models and technologies to analyze and portray image and video content for prediction and recognition. My research interests include, human gender recognition, image search, image and object classification, human action recognition. During my PhD period, my research interest will lies in machine learning, human-computer interaction, (deep) representation learning, robotics and their applications to computer vision.

 

My motto: If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough.
